About Butuan

Butuan City

Butuan City, located in the heart of Mindanao, is a captivating blend of history, culture, and natural beauty. As one of the Philippines' oldest cities, it offers a unique glimpse into the past with its archaeological sites, including the famous Balangay boats, which reveal the rich maritime history of the region. Butuan also thrives as a modern city with a vibrant local culture, lush landscapes, and a warm, welcoming atmosphere, making it the perfect destination for those seeking a deeper connection with both nature and heritage.
