Things to do while in Butuan

Guide for Tourists and Pilgrims

Balangay Shrine Museum

Best Time to Visit: The museum is open Monday – Friday, 8am – 5pm.

Butuan Cathedral

Best Time to Visit: Morning or Evening

Butuan City Plaza

Best Time to Visit: Early morning or late afternoon.

Mount Mayapay

Best Time to Visit: The best time to hike Mount Mayapay is during the dry season.

Agusan River

Best Time to Visit: Early morning or late afternoon.

National Museum of Butuan

Best Time to Visit: The museum is open Monday – Friday, 8am – 5pm.

Balangay Festival

Best Time to Visit: The Balangay Festival is held every May. If you’re looking for a lively cultural experience with street parades, dances, and local festivities, plan your trip around this vibrant event.

Public Market

Best Time to Visit: The market is busiest in the mornings (around 7 AM to 11 AM), when locals come to shop for fresh produce and goods. It’s also best to visit early to avoid the midday heat and to get the freshest selection of items.<

Bood Promontory Eco-Park

Best Time to Visit: The best time to visit the Butuan Eco-Park is during the cooler months of November to February. This is perfect for hiking, picnics, and enjoying the tranquil natural environment.

Cultural and Food Tours

Best Time to Visit: Food tours can be enjoyed year-round.

Banza Church Ruins

Best Time to Visit: Visit the Banza Church Ruins during the cooler parts of the day, either in the early morning or late afternoon. The site can be visited year-round, but it’s most enjoyable in the dry season.
