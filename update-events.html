Meet Me at the Stars – TikTok Dance Competition

Here’s how you can join the “Meet Me at the Stars” TikTok Dance Competition and have a chance to win amazing prizes:

  1. Create a TikTok Video: Showcase your dance moves to the trending official contest theme song: [APT – Rosè & Bruno Mars].
  2. Show the Stars: The entries of the Barangays for the Light-up A Star Competition are displayed at the City Hall Grounds (in front of the stage). These stars must be the background of your TikTok video.
  3. Follow the Page: Follow our Facebook Page - Butuan City Tourism before commenting on the post.
  4. Share the Post: Share the “MEET ME AT THE STARS – TIKTOK DANCE COMPETITION” post with the hashtags: #KasadyaHongPaskoNganiButuan #LightupAStar #MeetMeAtTheStars.
  5. Comment your Video on the Post: Post your TikTok video as a comment on the “Meet Me at the Stars” TikTok Dance Competition post. There will only be one (1) entry per person/group.
  6. Direct Message: Send a direct message to the official Butuan City Tourism page with a screenshot that you have commented the TikTok video, followed the page, and shared the post to ensure your video is counted as an official entry.
  7. Submission Deadline: Video entries through comments will be accepted from December 6 to December 20, 2024. Heart reactions will begin once posted and end at 5:00 PM on December 27, 2024. The awarding ceremony will take place during the Rizal Day program at 7:00 AM on December 30, 2024 at Guingona Park.
  8. Video Length: Your TikTok video must be between 30 seconds to 1 minute only.
  9. Be Creative and Original: Let your imagination run wild and create a unique and unforgettable performance.
  10. Prizes: The top 5 videos chosen will receive ₱5,000.00 each as a prize.
Judging Criteria
  • Overall Performance (40%): Refers to staging, spacing, formations, execution, showmanship, projection, gracefulness, and synchronization.
  • Video Presentation (30%): Refers to the overall quality and impact of your TikTok video, including clear video quality, creative camera angles, engaging visual effects, and smooth editing and transitions.
  • Social Media Impact (Heart Reacts Only) (20%): Refers to the number of heart reactions (only). Boosting or auto-like will disqualify the entry.
  • Costume/Attire (10%): Relevance to the theme, creativity, originality, and overall presentation of your costume/attire.

Make sure to follow all the steps to qualify and good luck in the competition! 🎉

Tourist Arrivals Report for Christmas Season 2024 - New Year 2025

As we anticipate an influx of tourists in Butuan City during the Christmas Season of 2024 through New Year 2025, we kindly request your cooperation in monitoring and submitting a report on tourist arrivals – foreign, domestic, and balikbayans. This is important for our data collection on tourists utilizing accommodation establishments for statistical purposes.

Tourist Arrivals Data Coverage

The data coverage period for the report will be from December 23, 2024 to January 2, 2025. We ask that you submit your report by January 6, 2025.

How to Submit Your Report

Please use the template accessible via the QR code below to submit your data. This will help us ensure that all necessary information is collected and processed efficiently.

Contact Information

Should you have any inquiries or require assistance, please do not hesitate to visit our office or contact:

  • Ms. Klarice C. Ty: 0916-411-3622
  • Email: ctcad@butuan.gov.ph

Your cooperation is highly appreciated, and we look forward to receiving your reports. Thank you for helping us gather crucial data for Butuan City's tourism statistics.
