BUTUAN: HOME OF THE BALANGAYS

WELCOME TO BUTUAN!

Welcome to Butuan City, a dynamic hub where the past and present seamlessly intertwine. Known as the "Historic City of the Philippines," Butuan proudly showcases its ancient roots with the legendary Balangay boats and archaeological treasures. Beyond its rich history, the city offers a thriving urban charm with diverse culinary delights, vibrant festivals, and welcoming locals.

Nature enthusiasts can explore scenic rivers, lush landscapes, and captivating sunsets, while adventurers can dive into the city’s outdoor experiences. Whether you're a history buff, foodie, or thrill-seeker, Butuan City promises unforgettable moments and stories to take home.
